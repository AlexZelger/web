How to Play

  1. Each round has a single stat category — Passing Yards, Rushing TDs, Receptions, and more.
  2. You'll get 5 slots. Each one shows a team or division with an optional year range.
  3. Search for a player that fits each slot's criteria.
  4. Pick the year you think they had their best performance.
  5. Submit all 5 picks. Your stat values are totalled into a final score.
